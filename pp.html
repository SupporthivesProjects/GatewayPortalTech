Data Management & Web Interaction Guidelines

Protecting your privacy is at the heart of our operations. This document communicates our commitment to safeguarding your personal details and explains how we handle your information in a secure and respectful manner.

Information We Collect

Gateway Portal Tech meticulously gathers specific data to enhance service delivery:

  • Identity Information: Including your full name and company name, facilitating a tailored and professional service experience.
  • Contact Information: Your email address and phone number are essential for direct and effective communication.
  • Usage Information: We analyse how you interact with our website to continually refine and elevate your experience.

Purpose and Use of Collected Data

We utilize your information with a clear purpose:

  • For the direct provision of our services, ensuring efficiency and personalization.
  • To maintain a seamless communication channel, keeping you informed and supported.
  • To understand service interaction patterns, guiding our enhancement efforts.

Cookie Insights

  • Definition and Purpose: Cookies, small text files on your device, are pivotal for gathering web interaction insights, aiding in service refinement and user experience enhancement.
  • User Control: You hold control over cookie settings. Adjust your browser configurations to manage cookie preferences, accepting or declining as you see fit.

Safeguarding Your Information

Gateway Portal Tech upholds stringent measures to protect your data from unauthorized intrusion, misuse, or potential risks, ensuring its integrity and confidentiality.

Data Usage Transparency

We prioritize transparency:

  • Your data is strictly for service enhancement and isn't traded or unjustly shared
  • Aggregate, non-identifiable data might be used collaboratively for analytics or improvement strategies.

Your Rights and Our Commitments

Under GDPR, your rights include:

  • Access and Control: Request access to or modification of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: The right to transfer your data.
  • Consent Revocation: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Right to be Forgotten: Request the deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Data Processing Justification: Our processing is based on explicit consent, contractual necessity, or legitimate interest, aligning with GDPR stipulations.

International Data Handling

For data that crosses borders:

  • We ensure that data transfers outside the EU adhere to legal standards and provide the necessary protection, resonating with GDPR's strict guidelines.

Updates to This Document

Gateway Portal Tech may modify these guidelines to stay aligned with the latest data protection practices. Regular reviews are recommended to stay informed about your data rights and our protective measures
